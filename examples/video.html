7 Principles To Mastering Growth Marketing

1.
Generalist or Specialist?

Master the Fundamentals: Data Analysis, Quantitative Modeling, User Psychology, Storytelling.

Then Go Deep, because “Being “ok” at a variety of things, is far less valuable than being amazing at one.”

2.
Choosing What You Work On

Optimize for learning. Go for high impact, low popularity (challenging) projects.

3.
Keep One Foot in the Known

Sit in the middle range between your Known (safe skills already mastered) and Unknown (completely new skills & terrain), have one foot in each.

4.
Accomplishments Over Credentials

Your accomplishments define you and your value (not credentials).

5.
Build a Platform

Build a platform that you own, so that: Opportunities Flow To You, You Create Leverage, You Build a Personal Brand.

6.
Get A Coach

Get a Coach that gives Unbiased Opinions and asks the Right Questions.

7.
How to be Proactive vs Reactive

Be Proactive (not Reactive), figure out “What do I really want?” through experiments.

Build A Growth Machine

1. Unlock growth for your business, product, or yourself

2. Learn how to never run out of new growth ideas

3. Learn how to rapidly test and analyze experiments
